Guojun YIN (殷国君)

Senior Researcher @ Meituan

I am currently a senior researcher at Meituan for the research and application of foundation models. Previously, I worked as a senior researcher at Huawei and a researcher at Sensetime Research. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from University of Science & Techonology of China (USTC) in 2019. During my graduate study, I spent a wonderful time at Multimedia Lab (MMLab), Chinese University of Hong Kong, from 2017 to 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaogang Wang . Before that, I received my bachelor's degree at University of Science & Techonology of China (USTC). My research interests focus on multimodal & foundation models and AIGC, as well as their applications in industry.
